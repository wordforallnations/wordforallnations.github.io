YOUTHGIG

YOUTHGIG is a youth organization that comes together
to share Jesus to a group of young people who have a common
goal of spreading the teachings and gospel of Jesus Christ.

Make friends.

We are an organizations that meet together F2F every Sunday
to learn and study His grace. We also conduct once a month
online youth convergence to meet up with other youth group in
our church. We hold events, camps and activities, that aimed at
educating, inspiring, and engaging young people in their
Christian faith.

Youth services and prayer gatherings to community service projects, retreats, and conferences. The focus of these organizations is typically to provide a supportive community for young people to grow in their faith and knowledge of His love, develop strong relationships with one another, and share the love of Jesus with those around them.

Get ready to join our awesome
collection of groups!
