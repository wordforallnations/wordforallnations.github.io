What to expect?

The WFAN Morning Devotion is a purposeful ministry that unites individuals in fellowship, all driven by the desire to understand God more deeply.

Guided by Paul Ellis' book "The Gospel In 10 Words," this ministry offers a shared journey of spiritual growth. Ellis' book distills profound insights about God's love and grace into just ten words, reshaping perspectives.

What are the benefits of joining the group?

Join the WFAN Morning Devotion group and experience a profound journey of spiritual growth and fellowship. Guided by Paul Ellis' transformative book "The Gospel In 10 Words," you'll uncover deep insights into God's love and grace.

Connect with a community of seekers, fostering authentic relationships and shared wisdom. Together, we illuminate our faith, uplift one another, and embark on a sacred path of collective spiritual exploration.

Where to join?

To join us, simply locate our gathering space at 8am on the 3rd floor of Fisher Mall, known as Cold Layers. Here, amidst a welcoming atmosphere, we delve into the WFAN Morning Devotion experience. Discover profound spiritual growth, guided by Paul Ellis' "The Gospel In 10 Words," and connect with a vibrant community of seekers. Your journey of fellowship and enlightenment begins at this uplifting location.
